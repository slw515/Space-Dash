Space Dash
About The Game

Space Dash was developed using Three JS to create the game design and mechanics as well as Posenet for accurate body position tracking. p5.js was used in order to render the camera as well as retrieve information from the Posenet library

How To Play

Stand in front of the camera and keep your shoulders (marked by the pink circles) in the pink rectangle! Make sure to enable your camera to play the game.

How To Play

Lean your torso to the left or to the right while keeping your legs straight to control the balls direction!

How To Play

Jump to make the ball jump!

How To Play

Avoid the red rings and hit the purple rings for extra points!

Game Over! Press the down key to play again!
